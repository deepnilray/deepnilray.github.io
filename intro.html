DEEPNIL RAY

Deputy CEO of NoRCEL | Asteroid Discoverer | Interdisciplinary Researcher

From discovering asteroid 2022 UO26 to leading international research at NoRCEL, I bridge astrophysics, astrobiology, AI ethics, and planetary science. My mission: exploring life's origins across the cosmos while reshaping education and fostering the next generation of boundary-pushing explorers.

Interdisciplinary Research

Astrobiology & Extremophiles

Investigating extremophiles and their role in understanding life's potential across the cosmos, bridging the gap between terrestrial biology and extraterrestrial possibilities.

Star Formation & Dynamics

Analyzing stellar birth processes and asteroid dynamics, contributing to our understanding of planetary system formation and evolution through observational astronomy.

AI Ethics & Autonomous Systems

Exploring ethical implications in artificial intelligence through works like "Ethics in Motion," examining care ethics in autonomous vehicles and responsible AI development.

Chemical Evolution of Life

Leading research initiatives at NoRCEL on the origins and evolution of life, fostering international collaboration in prebiotic chemistry and planetary stewardship.

Major Achievements

Asteroid Discovery: 2022 UO26

Officially discovered and named asteroid 2022 UO26, confirmed by NASA through the International Astronomical Search Collaboration (IASC) - a testament to dedication in observational astronomy and celestial mechanics.

ISRO & VSSC Fellowship

Selected for prestigious internships at Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre through highly competitive scholarship programs, contributing to India's space exploration mission.

International Recognition

National topper in multiple scientific competitions, honors in global math and science Olympiads, and telescope image processing for Institute of Astrophysics of Canary Islands, featured in Open University publications.

Harvard-Smithsonian Collaboration

Active participant in Harvard-Smithsonian's DIY Planet Search program and TESS data analysis, contributing to exoplanet discovery and characterization efforts at the forefront of astronomical research.

Leadership & Impact

Deputy CEO of NoRCEL

Leading the Network of Researchers on Chemical Evolution of Life, an international UK-based organization fostering cross-disciplinary collaboration in origin-of-life studies and sustainable planetary stewardship.

Science Communication

Authored children's books about space travel, recorded audiobooks for visually impaired individuals, and actively engages global scientific communities through multiple platforms including ResearchGate, SSRN, and academic publications.

Educational Innovation

Championing passion-driven education models that break free from rigid curricula, empowering early-career researchers through editorial publications and fellowship programs that foster interdisciplinary exploration.

Published Research

Academic writings published through prestigious platforms including SSRN, Springer, and Elsevier, bridging scientific analysis with philosophical and ethical inquiry across multiple disciplines.

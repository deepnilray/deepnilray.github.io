What is an Exoplanet?

An exoplanet is a planet that exists outside of our solar system, orbiting a star other than the Sun. These mysterious worlds are at the forefront of astronomical research, and many are being discovered each year!

  • HD 209458b: The first exoplanet discovered with an atmosphere.
